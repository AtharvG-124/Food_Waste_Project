What Even is this Place?

As of now, this place is an awareness website. Many people don't know the big impact food waste is making. I also was oblivious to the statistics but as I searched about it more, I started to realize that we just look over the food that we waste.



Future Plans

This website will be more than just an awareness site. I want to continue on and make this a place where people can donate food and other people can accept that food. I also want to spread the statistics that I was oblivious to, so people can do something about it. There will also be a blog page where people can share their thoughts and come up with ideas that can remove the impediment called food waste.
