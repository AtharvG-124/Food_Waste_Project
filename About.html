My Train of Thought


The prompt given was "Solve a World Problem." The first thoughts that came to mind was 'global warming' and 'poverty.' These problems would be hard to solve with a computer program, so I researched about other problems out there and found out that Food Waste was a big problem. I knew that world hunger and garbage was a big problem. I felt bad about it but as time passed, I got involved with other things and slowly forgot about it.

A feeling of guilt seized me, and I decided that I would make an awareness website about this topic.



Inspiration

As I searched for statistics, I stumbled upon this video by Vox (it's below). The ideas and information in the video boosted my confidence,and I decided to make a prototype of an app that would prevent waste at home.
